About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Indiana University, Bloomington, with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, and am planning to graduate in the Fall of 2021. I previously studied at Indiana University-Purdue University Indiana (IUPUI) for multiple years, working towards a Bachelor of Science in Media Arts and Science, and a minor in Informatics. In that degree, I studied Full-Stack Web Development and gained knowledge in both front-end and back-end web systems. For my minor in Informatics, I studied Information Infrastructure, and Human-Computer Interaction. I am a hardworking individual, having earned Dean's List multiple years in a row, and having participated in the Applied Computing Club at IUPUI along with my studies.

I am experienced in multiple aspects of web development and software development. My current technical skills include Java, Python, PHP, Javascript, C, C#, HTML, CSS, SCSS, jQuery, React, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and SQLite. I have also learned and used Android Studio in the development of multiple Android apps in my studies. I have learned, and applied, concepts such as Object Oriented Programming, and Model-View-Controller design for creating websites, and other forms of software such as Android apps.

My major specialization had given me experience with multiple types of Machine Learning algorithms and methods, including neural networks, support-vector machines, deep learning, and k-clusters. I also took class work in the Software Engineering and Data Analysis specializations.
